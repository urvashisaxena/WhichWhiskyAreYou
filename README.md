## Features

### тЬЕ Full spell check
- Built-in **368,000-word English dictionary** (bundled in `dictionary.js`)
- "Did you mean?" suggestions generated by edit-distance search, ranked by word frequency and typo patterns (e.g. `yor` тЖТ *your*, `recieved` тЖТ *received*, `detales` тЖТ *details*)
- Missing-apostrophe fixes: `dont` тЖТ *don't*, `im` тЖТ *I'm*, `youre` тЖТ *you're*
- Smart about what **not** to flag: proper nouns, acronyms, URLs, email addresses, camelCase, and contractions like *y'all* and *o'clock*
- **Personal dictionary**: click "+ Dictionary" on any flagged word to never see it flagged again (saved in your browser's localStorage)

### ЁЯЫа Editor
- Live checking as you type, with colored underlines and matching suggestion cards
- **Apply** any fix with one click, **Fix all safe issues** at once, or **Dismiss** suggestions you disagree with
- Click a suggestion card to jump to that spot in your text

- Category filters, sample text demo, copy/clear buttons, and dark mode

## Privacy

There is nothing to sign up for and nothing is collected. All checking happens in JavaScript on your machine. The only thing stored is your personal dictionary and theme preference, in your own browser's localStorage.

## How the spell checker works

1. Every word is looked up in the bundled dictionary (generated from [dwyl/english-words](https://github.com/dwyl/english-words), Unlicense, with a modern-vocabulary supplement and a curated short-word whitelist).
2. Unknown words get suggestions by generating every string within one edit (insertion, deletion, replacement, transposition) тАФ and two edits for short words тАФ then keeping the ones that are real words.
3. Candidates are ranked by frequency (from [google-10000-english](https://github.com/first20hours/google-10000-english)) plus typo heuristics: misspellings rarely change a word's first or last letter.

A 5,000-word document checks in about 25 ms.
